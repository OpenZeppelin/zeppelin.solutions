Marketing Lead for South Korea

As Zeppelin’s community grows, we want to increase our commitment in South Korea with a local Marketing Lead. You’ll help us connect to the communities that love the decentralized web as much as we do.

You are a dynamic and encouraging individual with a passion to bring people together to develop decentralized technologies. You are energetic, organized and detail-oriented, with the ability to adapt to fast-paced environments. You’ll work remotely with our international team, with the direct support of our Head of Marketing.

What you'll do

  • Build content distribution, communication and community strategies for South Korea together with our Marketing team and PR agencies.

  • Plan, coordinate and organize meetups, hackathons and other events in Asia.

  • Source, cover and evaluate sponsorships for events in Asia.

  • Foster a healthy local community in Telegram, Wechat and other channels by sourcing supporting community managers.

  • Create new targeted content, translate the existing one to Korean and other languages if necessary.

  • Build partnerships with relevant projects with a local presence.

Who you are

  • An excellent communicator, with proficient written and spoken communication skills in English and Korean.

  • An individual experienced in working remotely with international teams.

  • An active member or contributor to different local events, meetups and community channels.

  • A passionate relationship-builder with strong networking skills.

  • An organized, data-driven professional with a love for self time management.

Nice-to-haves

  • Previous experience contributing or managing crypto or open source projects.

  • Technical understanding of blockchain technologies.

  • Lived in South Korea for some time during the past 3 years.

  • Spent 2+ years living or studying in the US or other Western countries.

Apply

Send an email to jobs@zeppelin.solutions with the phrase "Marketing Lead for South Korea" on the subject line.

Attach CV or LinkedIn profile with references to previous content, communication, and community projects.

Bonus: Let us know why Zeppelin caught your attention!
