Technical Writer

Job Type: Contractor

Location: Remote / Buenos Aires

Zeppelin builds the industry security standard for architecting and deploying smart contract systems in public blockchains. We build open source infrastructure and conduct in-depth system security audits to high-impact smart contract systems. Learn more about us!

In an open source, fast-paced yet young ecosystem, Technical Writers play a big role enabling knowledge scalability. We’re looking for a Technical Writer to help us build the foundations of OpenZeppelin and ZeppelinOS: the documentation.

As a Technical Writer, you will communicate OpenZeppelin and ZeppelinOS in a clear, concise and accurate manner. You’ll be working closely with our product and engineering team and third-party developers. Zeppelin is an engineering company. We look for talented people but above that, we seek for passionate, proactive and autonomous individuals to join our team!

What you'll do

  • Write technical documentation and developer guides for internal and external developers.

  • Design, publish and maintain documentation sets for OpenZeppelin and ZeppelinOS.

  • Support developer documentation with sample code.

  • Collaborate with the product and engineering teams to identify and address third-party developers needs.

  • Write, edit and publish online articles, presentation and other written and visual content for internal and external developers.

Who we're looking for

  • Relevant experience in technical writing, online publishing or creating software documentation.

  • Some engineering experience with web, software or API development or integrating third-party apps.

  • Excellent verbal and written English communication skills.

  • Passion for decentralized and/or open-source technologies and advocating them to others.

  • An organized, data-driven professional with a love for self-time management.

Nice to have

  • Previous experience contributing or managing crypto/open-source projects.
