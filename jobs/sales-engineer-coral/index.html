Sales Engineer - Coral

Job Type: Full Time

Location: Remote / Buenos Aires

Zeppelin builds the industry security standard for architecting and deploying smart contract systems in public blockchains. We build open source infrastructure and conduct in-depth system security audits to high-impact smart contract systems. Learn more about us!

Coral is a fundraiser platform for Ethereum tokens available for anyone in the world, without writing a single line of code. We’re looking for a Sales Engineer to help us meet Coral clients’ needs.

Our Sales Engineer will combine technical knowledge with sales skills to leverage Coral’s impact. You’ll talk to engineers, engage with clients and build relationships with the emerging blockchain community. You’ll work with an amazing engineering and business development team lowering the barrier to entry to the blockchain ecosystem.

What you'll do

  • Work with the business development team to build and implement specific Coral solutions.

  • Lead technical sales calls.

  • Generate, evaluate and engage new sales opportunities.

Who we're looking for

  • Degree in a technical discipline preferred.

  • Some knowledge of smart contracts development.

  • Excellent presentation, verbal and written English communication skills.

  • Passion for decentralized and/or open-source technologies and advocating them to others.

  • An organized, data-driven professional with a love for self-time management.

Nice-to-haves

  • Previous experience contributing or managing crypto or open source projects.

  • Technical understanding of blockchain technologies.

  • Previous experience working with sales or business development departments.

  • Previous experience with smart contract development.

How to apply

Complete the following Application Form

If the Application Form is not working, send an email to jobs@zeppelin.solutions with the phrase "Sales Engineer Coral" on the subject line.
