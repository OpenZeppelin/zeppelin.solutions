Join Zeppelin

Help us build the foundations of the decentralized web

Software Engineer

You have vision, innovation, passion, technical ability, a drive to deliver, as well as collaboration and execution. You are not risk averse and want to build products that will change the world.

Get a first hand view of what it takes to build and deliver software in the blockchain technical security space and help us build and protect the infrastructure of a new economy.

Requirements

  • BS/MS in Computer Science or similar technical subject.

  • Self-starter, able to work on his own initiative with minimal supervision.

  • Deep understanding of blockchain and distributed ledger protocols and smart contract scripting languages (e.g. Solidity).

  • Ability to apply technical theories, principles and techniques into real world applications.

  • Active demonstrable interest in blockchain technology, distributed systems, DHTs and cryptography.

  • Keep up to date on emerging capabilities in this fast growing area and identify where and how new capabilities can be applied.

  • Think through and anticipate how attackers and others may attempt to subvert or mis-use applications.

  • Enthusiasm for working in a space full of unknown unknowns, pivoting as needed, and being comfortable with not knowing the road to the end goal.

  • Candidate must be interested in working in a collaborative environment and possess good client interfacing skills, good communication skills and able to work in both a team environment as well as individually.

Preferred qualifications

  • Previous work with P2P Networking and distributed systems.

  • Understanding of various distributed algorithms (mining, PoS, PoW, PoA).

  • Understanding of cryptography, including asymmetric (pub/priv key), symmetric, hash functions, encryption/signatures. (ECDH, ECDSA, PGP and AES).

Apply

Send an email to jobs@zeppelin.solutions with the phrase "Software Engineer" on the subject line.
