Head of Marketing & Communications

Zeppelin builds the industry security standard for architecting and deploying smart contract systems in public blockchains. We build open source infrastructure and conduct in-depth system security audits to high-impact smart contract systems. Learn more about us!

As a project working with cutting-edge technology, building a strong brand and marketing strategy is essential to effectively reach the right audience. We’re looking for a Marketing Director to help us build and implement Zeppelin’s global marketing strategy.

Our Head of Marketing & Communications will define our branding, communication and community strategies and lead the international expansion efforts together with the founders and the global community and expansion managers.

What you'll do

  • Creating and implementing Zeppelin Solution’s brand, communication and community strategies focused on developers, partners and hiring.

  • Finding, curating & creating content for Zeppelin distributions channels (Twitter, Slack, Email, Reddit) and PR.

  • Track, measure and optimize our communication channels and impact for different stakeholders.

  • Source, plan, and coordinate meetups, hackathons, and other events around the world.

  • Finding speaking opportunities at conferences and define actions to increase community engagement.

  • Help source and hire community and expansion managers in China/Hong Kong, South Korea, and other locations.

Who we're looking for

  • You have previous experience building international marketing strategies in technology companies.

  • You have a deep understanding of the blockchain space and technology.

  • You have the ability to design and execute a marketing strategy under tight deadlines, coordinating with different stakeholders during the process (project management).

  • You have excellent writing skills and experience creating, editing and promoting written and visual content.

  • You have a good eye for design and you'd like to help on website optimization.

  • You're a strong communicator, project manager, and problem solver.

  • You have strong interpersonal skills, and you’re organized, data-driven with a love for self-time management.

Nice-to-haves

  • Previous experience contributing or managing crypto or open source projects.

  • Some technical understanding of blockchain technologies.
