Developer Relations

Job Type: Full Time

Location: Remote / Buenos Aires

Zeppelin builds the industry security standard for architecting and deploying smart contract systems in public blockchains. We build open source infrastructure and conduct in-depth system security audits to high-impact smart contract systems. Learn more about us!

In an open source, fast-paced yet early space, Developer Relations play a big role bridging the gap between Zeppelin’s product and the ecosystem’s needs. We’re looking for a Developer Relations to help us evangelise and advocate OpenZeppelin and ZeppelinOS.

Our Developer Relations will be the technical reference for third-party developers building with Zeppelin’s infrastructure. You’ll talk to respected engineers, engage the community, build relationships and support dapps developers. You’ll work with an amazing product, marketing and engineering team fighting for a more accessible and secure public blockchain infrastructure from the frontline.

What you'll do

  • Spread awareness, engage and grow a community of developers and contributors for OpenZeppelin and ZeppelinOS.

  • Attend, cover and present at conferences, hackathons, meetups and other events.

  • Design, improve and maintain a bounties program.

  • Design, write and maintain documentation sets for internal and external developer for OpenZeppelin and ZeppelinOS.

  • Collaborate with the product and engineering teams to identify and address third-party developers needs; help define product features and roadmap.

  • Write, edit and publish online articles, presentation and other written and visual content for internal and external developers.

Who we're looking for

  • Provable experience doing technical presentations, contributing to meetups or organizing events for developers.

  • Some engineering experience with web, software or API development or integrating third-party apps.

  • Excellent public speaking, verbal and written English communication skills.

  • Passion for decentralized and/or open-source technologies and advocating them to others.

  • An organized, data-driven professional with a love for self-time management.

Nice-to-haves

  • Previous experience contributing or managing crypto or open source projects.

  • Previous experience in technical writing, online publishing or creating software documentation.
