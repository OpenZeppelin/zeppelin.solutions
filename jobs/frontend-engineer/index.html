Frontend Engineer

Job Type: Full Time

Location: Global

Zeppelin builds the industry security standard for architecting and deploying smart contract systems in public blockchains. We build open source infrastructure and conduct in-depth system security audits to high-impact smart contract systems. Learn more about us!

The key to adoption is creating the best UI/UX experience. We’re looking for a Frontend Engineer to help us connect smart contracts with our users.

Our Frontend Engineer will create the interfaces of the applications we build as well as sample Dapps to showcase our tools.

What you'll do

  • Research, build and test user facing interfaces for Open Zeppelin and Zeppelin OS.

  • Experiment and develop user interfaces for sample and experimental apps built on top of Open Zeppelin and Zeppelin OS.

  • Work closely with the product and engineering teams to define and implement new product features.

  • Collaborate with our designer to develop templates, usability improvements and functionality to our websites.

Who you are

  • You have expert understanding of HTML and CSS.

  • You have knowledge in Javascript and React.

  • You have extensive experience with web design, web application and UI engineering.

  • You have knowledge of Git.

  • You have proven ability to ship high-quality, well-crafted code within tight deadlines.

  • You put a focus on details and design.

  • You're an autonomous individual with a proactive attitude.

  • You have advanced english communication skills- most of our internal and external communication is in English.

  • You may have previous experience contributing in open source projects.

  • You may have technical understanding of blockchain concepts.
